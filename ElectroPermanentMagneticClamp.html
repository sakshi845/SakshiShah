Two Pole Electro-Permanent Magnet Clamp for Workpiece holding during machining

This project optimized a two-pole electro-permanent magnetic clamping system designed to replace traditional clamps in machining operations during machining processes. Iterative design and analysis using Magnetic Circuit Analysis (MCA), Finite Element Method Magnetics (FEMM), and ANSYS software ensured the achievement of performance goals.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, OpenCV, GStreamer, ROS, Kinova Gen 3 Robot, Data-Driven Controllers
