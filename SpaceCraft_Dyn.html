Spacecraft Attitude Dynamics of Low Orbit Satellites

This project involved re-deriving and analyzing aircraft dynamics equations based on the paper "Modeling and Numerical Simulation of Linear and Nonlinear Spacecraft Attitude Dynamics and Gravity Gradient Moments: A Comparative Study" by M. Navabi, N. Nasiri, and Mehdi Dehghan.

  • Equation Standardization & Validation: Re-derive core equations using MAE511 notation to ensure consistency across literature and serve as an error-checking mechanism.
  • Simulation & Error Analysis: Simulate the equations to evaluate errors introduced by linear approximations, particularly under the small angle assumption.
  • Model Comparison & Validation: Assess the divergence between linear and nonlinear models, establishing an upper bound for the validity of the small angle assumption.
Concepts Learned:
Euler Equations, Frame Transformations, ODE-45
Software Used:
MATLAB
