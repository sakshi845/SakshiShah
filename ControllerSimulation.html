Tracking Control of a Robot Manipulator with Uncertainties

This project focuses on understanding the differences between various nonlinear control design techniques. The primary objective is to design, simulate, and compare the performance of three distinct nonlinear controllers for a tracking task:

  • Exact Model Knowledge (EMK)
  • Adaptive Control
  • Robust Control
    • It involves deriving the governing equations and representing them using the notations introduced in the BME 590 course with a detailed analysis of each control technique, evaluating its stability and tracking accuracy

    Technologies Used: MATLAB, OpenCV, GStreamer, ROS, Kinova Gen 3 Robot, Data-Driven Controllers
